THE WEDDING

Join us as we celebrate our marriage at the Captain's House, in Chester, Nova Scotia. The Captain’s House is a re-modelled sea-captain's house, with plenty of room for mingling, dancing and games. Think of it as a really big house party!.

CEREMONY 3:00PM

Weather permitting, the ceremony will take place outside on the patio of The Captain’s House.
The Captain’s House
33 Central Street
Chester, NS

****RAIN PLAN**** If the weather doesn’t cooperate, the ceremony will move a few blocks away to Tuck Hall at St. Stephen’s Parish. A decision will be made the day of and a sign will be posted in front of The Captain’s House confirming the ceremony location.
Tuck Hall / St. Stephen’s Parish Centre
54 Regent Street
Chester, NS

54 Regent Street
Chester, NS

RECEPTION 9:00PM

After dinner guests can make their way back to The Captain’s House for drinks, dancing and merriment. Note the bar is cash only.

Peggy's Cove

Explore the rocky shores of Peggy’s Cove. This is one of the most iconic spots in Nova Scotia, even as locals we make the trip out a few times a year and it never disappoints. Stay off the wet rocks and bring warm clothes!

In the fall we love to bundle up and walk along the beaches, a few of our favorites on the South Shore are: Hirtles Beach and Hiking Trail, Crescent Beach (you can drive your car right onto the sand) and Queensland Beach.
